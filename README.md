This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance and profitability for a multi-channel business (Brick & Mortar and E-commerce). By transforming raw transactional data from 2017 to 2020 into interactive visualizations, the dashboard enables stakeholders to identify revenue drivers, track regional performance, and optimize profit margins.

* **Core KPI Tracking:** Monitored total **Revenue (‚Çπ142M)**, **Sales Quantity (350K)**, and **Total Profit Margin (‚Çπ2.1M)**.
* **Market Dominance:** Identified **Delhi NCR** as the primary revenue hub, contributing **54.7%** of total revenue.
* **Profitability Paradox:** Discovered that while Delhi leads in volume, regions like **Bhubaneshwar** exhibit higher relative profit percentages (**10.5%**), highlighting opportunities for operational efficiency.
* **Customer & Product Intelligence:** Isolated the **Top 5 Customers** (led by Electricalsara Stores at ‚Çπ66M) and **Top 5 Products** to understand brand loyalty and inventory demand.
* **Revenue Trends:** Visualized a significant downward trend in revenue toward the end of Q2 2020, providing a basis for market recovery analysis.
